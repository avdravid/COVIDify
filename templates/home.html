COVID-19 Lung Scanner

This application uses a generative adversarial network (GAN) to overlay COVID-19 features onto X-ray images of healthy lungs. We believe that, by isolating the additional features generated by the GAN, we may potentially identify specific physiological changes both during the disease and post-recovery. We hope to extend this platform to novel diseases as they appear in order to more quickly assess their long-term effects on internal organs.
